# CosmoWAP Papers

Reference papers for the CosmoWAP package.

## 2407.00168.pdf
**Multipoles of the galaxy bispectrum on a light cone: wide-separation and relativistic corrections**
Addis, Guandalin & Clarkson (2025), JCAP 04 (2025) 080
*Original CosmoWAP paper - lays out wide-separation  (wide-angle, radial-redshift) formalism and includes relativistic corrections to the bispectrum.*

## 2511.09466.pdf
**Unbiased analysis of primordial non-Gaussianity: the multipoles of the full relativistic power spectrum**
Addis, Guedezounme, Hammond, Clarkson, Montano, Camera, Jolicoeur & Maartens (2025)
*Integrated contributions contributions to the power spectrum and (multi-tracer) forecasts on PNG*
